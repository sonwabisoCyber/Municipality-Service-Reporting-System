Welcome to Emfuleni Municipality Service Reporting

The Municipality Service Reporting System is a secure web-based platform that allows residents and municipality staff to access municipal services online. Residents can report service delivery issues, submit requests, and monitor the progress of their reports, while municipality staff can efficiently review, manage, and resolve reported cases. The system improves communication, increases transparency, reduces paperwork, and supports faster service delivery across the Emfuleni Local Municipality.
