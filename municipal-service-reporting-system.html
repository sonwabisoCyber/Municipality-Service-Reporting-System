Welcome to Emfuleni Municipality Service Reporting

The Municipality Service Reporting System is a web-based application designed to improve communication between residents and the municipality. The system provides a secure login and registration platform where users can access services based on their roles, such as municipality staff or residents. Residents can report service delivery issues, submit requests, and track the progress of their reports, while municipality staff can manage, review, and resolve reported issues efficiently. By digitizing the reporting process, the system reduces paperwork, improves response times, enhances transparency, and enables better service delivery to the community.
